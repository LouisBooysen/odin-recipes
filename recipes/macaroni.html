Macaroni recipe

Description

This is traditional itallian dish

This is how you can ake it easily at Home.

Ingredients:

  • 6 ounces macaroni
  • water to cover
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 ounce heavy cream
  • 6 ounces grated Colby cheese

Step:

  1. Place a medium skillet over medium-high heat. Pour in dry macaroni noodles. Pour in enough water to barely cover macaroni. Bring to a boil,stirring continuously.
  2. Continue stirring the macaroni until water is almost evaporated and pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Pour in evaporated milk and cream. Add grated cheese and stir until cheese has melted and everything is well combined. Serve immediately.
